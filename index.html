The Problem

Municipal Systems Are Broken

Legacy governance models suffer from deep inefficiencies that hinder service delivery and erode public trust.

👻

Proxy Attendance & Ghost Workers

Fraudulent attendance marking and ghost employees drain municipal budgets without any service delivery, with no way to verify physical presence.

📡

No Real-Time Field Monitoring

Admins have zero visibility into where field workers are at any given moment, making it impossible to ensure tasks are being performed on time.

🔍

Delayed & Unverifiable Service Delivery

Task completion reports cannot be verified. Citizens suffer from delayed waste collection, broken roads, and unresolved drainage issues with no accountability trail.

What is CivicTrack

The operating system for accountable city governance.

CivicTrack transforms field operations into a fully digital, trackable, and auditable ecosystem. From garbage collection to road repairs — every task is verified, timestamped, and geotagged.

How It Works

From Field to Dashboard

A seamless 5-step digital workflow that ensures every task is tracked, verified, and accountable.

📱

Worker Opens App

Field worker launches the CivicTrack mobile app to start their shift

📍

GPS Check-In

Location verified via geofencing — attendance only marks within designated zone

📋

Task Assigned

Admin pushes tasks digitally with priority, location, and time targets

📸

Photo Proof

Worker submits geotagged, watermarked photo as irrefutable proof of completion

✅

Verified & Logged

Admin reviews and data flows into the dashboard in real time

Sustainability

Aligned with the UN SDGs

CivicTrack isn't just a governance tool — it's a commitment to a sustainable, equitable urban future.

SDG 6

Clean Water & Sanitation

Precise tracking of sanitation workers ensures clogged drains and waste issues are resolved on time, preventing disease and promoting public health.

SDG 11

Sustainable Cities

Continuous infrastructure monitoring ensures cities stay functional, clean, and resilient — improving quality of life for all residents.

SDG 12

Responsible Consumption

Full digitization eliminates paper-based records and manual registers, drastically reducing material consumption and administrative waste.

SDG 13

Climate Action

Proximity-based task assignment minimizes unnecessary vehicle travel, reducing fuel consumption and lowering the municipal carbon footprint.
